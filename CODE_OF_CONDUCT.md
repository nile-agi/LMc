# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

LMc exists to make AI accessible to everyone — including people in places that most AI projects
forget. We are building for low-spec phones, aging laptops, limited bandwidth, and communities
that have been underserved by technology. That mission requires a community where everyone feels
safe to contribute, ask questions, and learn.

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in LMc a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, experience level,
nationality, gender identity, or religion.

---

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that builds a healthy community:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing technical opinions
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- Showing empathy toward other contributors, especially those who are newer or less experienced

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing contributions because of where someone is from or what device they use
- Trolling, deliberately unhelpful responses, or bad-faith criticism
- Publishing private information about others without their consent

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a private issue or contacting
the maintainers directly (see the README for contact details). All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers have the right to remove comments, close issues, and ban contributors who violate
this code of conduct.
